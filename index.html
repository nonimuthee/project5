EAST AFRICA

Cuisines you MUST try when you travel to East Africa

We are going to explore East African cuisine through a fun and heartwarming experience with Mark Wiens.

Eating With My Dad - Traditional East African Food

If there's one person who knows how to make you hungry through a screen, it’s Mark Wiens! His passion for food is contagious, and in this video, he shares a heartwarming meal with his dad.

From Ugali to Sukuma Wiki, these are the dishes that bring families together. If you've ever wondered what a home-cooked East African meal feels like, this is your chance to experience it—just don’t blame me if you start craving food halfway through!

Source: YouTube

Arab Kenyan Food - Coconut Grilled Fish

Kenya’s coastal food scene is where the Swahili and Arab worlds collide in the most delicious way possible. In this video, Mark Wiens teams up with Chef Ali Mandhry to dive into the magic of Mombasa’s coconut-grilled fish.

Imagine fresh seafood marinated with coconut milk, infused with bold spices, and grilled to perfection. You can almost smell the ocean breeze and feel the warmth of Swahili hospitality while watching this. Absolute food heaven!

Source: YouTube

The Ultimate Kenyan Street Food Tour

If you’ve never experienced Kenyan street food, you are seriously missing out! Lucky for us, Mark Wiens has done the ultimate deep dive into Mombasa’s food scene, tasting some of the best coastal dishes.

From smoky Nyama Choma (grilled meat) to sweet Mahamri (fried dough), every dish tells a story. The street vendors, the sizzling grills, and the vibrant culture—it’s all here! Be warned: you might find yourself Googling flights to Kenya after watching this.
